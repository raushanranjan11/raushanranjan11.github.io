About Us

Tiny Treasures Preschool (Powered by KREEDO Early Childhood Solutions) was established in November 2019 with a vision to provide an environment that is full of acceptance, freedom for exploration and love for learning.

Taking baby steps, it has expanded its wings over the years understanding, experiencing and loving children. Our vision is to ensure that children enjoy their childhood and they learn, play, explore, discover and grow without succumbing to the pressures of growing up. A beautiful combination of playschool, day care and a kids activity club, it serves as a one stop destination for children from 2+ years.

Program

The Pre-Montessori course has been designed to meet the education and exploration needs of children between 2 yrs and 6 yrs. It supports the physical, cognitive, social and emotional development of each child. The program strengthens speech and language development and both fine and gross motor skills as the child explores self, family and the world in which they live.
